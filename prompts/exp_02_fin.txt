Objective:
Discover and evaluate quantitative long-short equity strategies using daily OHLCV data.

Data:
- Use local OHLCV CSV files stored on disk
- Data location: ./data/ohlcv/
- One CSV file per asset
- Columns: date, open, high, low, close, volume
- Do NOT download data from the internet

Universe:
- US equities
- Daily frequency

Strategy type:
- Market-neutral long/short
- Dollar neutral
- Monthly rebalancing

Baseline:
- Equal-weighted 20-day momentum strategy

Factor generation:
- Generate interpretable alpha factors based on:
  - momentum
  - mean reversion
  - volatility
  - volume
- Avoid overly complex expressions

Backtest protocol:
- Train: 2016–2020
- Validation: 2021–2022
- Test: 2023–2024
- Transaction cost: 10 bps per trade
- Penalize monthly turnover above 40%

Evaluation metrics:
- Annualized return
- Annualized volatility
- Sharpe ratio
- Max drawdown
- Monthly turnover

Optimization goal:
- Improve Sharpe ratio on test vs baseline
- Keep drawdown and turnover under control

Output:
1. Top 3 discovered factors with intuition
2. Performance comparison vs baseline
3. Equity curves
4. Discussion of robustness
5. Final recommended strategy
